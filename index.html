Styles of Horse Riding + Types of Horses Used

Western

Western riding came from ranching and warfare traditions. Western riding can also be a competitive sport. Stock type horses are used for this kind of riding.

Stock Horses (Western type horses)

English

English riding is more elegant than Western and is the most commonly seen type of riding. English riding is also a competitive sport. Hunter, Pleasure and Saddle type horses are used for this type of riding.

Pleasure, Hunter and Saddle Horses (English type Horses)
